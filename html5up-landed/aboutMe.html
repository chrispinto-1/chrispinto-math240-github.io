Bio

Hello! My name is Chris Pinto and I am a rising junior at the University of Pennsylvania. I'm an Uncoordinated Dual Degree student in Wharton and the School of Engineering studying Finance and Bioengineering.

I'm from Yorktown Heights, New York but I will be teaching from Philly this semester! My biggest passion is skiing, ever since my parents sent me down the slopes at 4 years old. I love hiking and playing the guitar too!

For on-campus activites, you may see me tour guiding on Locust Walk. I'm also a part of the Biomedical Engineering Society, Moelis Access to Science, and Access Engineering.

My current research interests are in computational orthopedics and biomechanics. I'm currently working on a project creating models to predict patient outcomes from greater tuberosity fractures by performing computational impingement assays. Pretty much I find bones pretty cool.

I'm excited to be your TA this semester and get to know all of you! See you over zoom!
